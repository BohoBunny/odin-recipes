Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti sauce uses ground beef, San Marzano tomatoes, italian seasoning, sweet onions and balsamic vinegar to create the perfect meat sauce blend. Use thin spaghetti noodles for a lighter meal. This spaghetti is southern style than traditional italian, you can add 2 teaspoons chile pepper flakes for a more traditional taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 sweet onion, minced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp. oregano
  • 2 tsp. basil
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • (16 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 Tbsp. sugar
  • (16 oz.) package/home made spaghetti, cooked and warm to serve

Steps

  1. Add beef and onions to a large pan or dutch oven, over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook beef and onions until brown and tender while breaking into small pieces.
  3. Strain beef draining off any grease.
  4. Stir in garlic, oregano, and basil, Cooking for 1 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. add in tomato sauce and sugar.
  7. Bring to a low boil then let simmer for 30 to 40 minutes.
  8. Add balsamic vinegar to taste.
