Bourbon Chicken

Description

Bourbon Chicken is a staple in southern cuisine from Virginia to Louisiana its a family favorite that graces most tables. With sweet brown sugar, savory soy sauce, iconic bourbon his quick and easy dish is sure to have everyone wanting seconds without breaking the bank.

This bourbon Chicken uses real bourbon for taste only and chicken thighs to get a more tender meat. If you want a more spicy dish you can add old bay to sub for red peppers and Sriracha sauce. While not required a Wok works best for this kind of dish. If you don't have a wok or dutch oven you can you a large frying pan instead.
