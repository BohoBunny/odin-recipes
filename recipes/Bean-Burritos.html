Bean Burritos

Description

These Bean burritos are stuffed with rich refried beans, and fluffy rice they're perfect for a quick and easy lunch or dinner. Along with being quick and easy burritos are also extremely customizable to ones preference. If you have any extra vegetables or sour cream you can add them into this recipe or use to garnish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can (16 ounces)refried-beans
  • 1 Cup salsa
  • 1 Cup cooked long grain rice
  • 2 Cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 12 Flour tortillas (6 inches)
  • (12oz) Shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
  2. In a large bowl, combine beans, salsa, rice and 1 cup cheese.
  3. Spoon about 1/3 cup mixture off-center on each tortilla.
  4. Fold the sides and ends over filling and roll up.
  5. Arrange burritos in a greased 13x9-in baking dish.
  6. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup cheese.
  7. Cover and bake until heated through, 20-25 minutes.
  8. Top with lettuce or desired topping.
